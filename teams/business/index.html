Business

Sponsorship

In order for anything we do to be possible, we require funding from all of our generous sponsors. The sponsorship department of the business subteam is responsible for reaching out to our current sponsors and ensuring their continued support. The business team also works to form new relationships with interested sponsors. MAAV thanks all its sponsors greatly for their contributions over the years.

Blog

Our blog on Medium is a great way to keep up with everything that MAAV does. It is meant as a resource both for members of MAAV to understand what the other subteams are doing, and for non-members to understand what the club is doing. It will be updated approximately every month, with a post detailing all the activites of each of the individual subteams, to give people a good idea of our big-picture workings.
